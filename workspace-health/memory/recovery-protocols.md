## Protocol Library

### Cognitive Recovery (after heavy decision sessions)
*Default protocol (evidence-based baseline):*
- **Minimum:** 20-minute walk, no screens, no decision-making
- **Preferred:** 90-minute sleep extension the following night
- **Full recovery:** 24-hour period with no complex decisions

### Acute Stress Response
*When [SAGE: REST] is triggered during a session:*
- **Immediate:** 5-minute box breathing (4s in / 4s hold / 4s out / 4s hold)
- **Short-term:** Step away from the session for 15 minutes minimum
- **If persistent:** End session. Return tomorrow.

### Decision Fatigue Protocol
*When [SAGE: DEFER] is triggered on an irreversible decision:*
- Write down the decision and the current options
- Close the session
- Sleep on it — return with fresh context
- Research shows decision quality on complex irreversible choices improves after sleep in >80% of cases
