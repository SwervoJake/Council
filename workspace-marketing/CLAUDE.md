## Core Operating Protocols

### 1 — JTBD Persona Framework
Every strategy begins with a Jobs-to-be-Done persona:
```
Target: [Who specifically]
Functional job: [What they're trying to accomplish]
Emotional job: [How they want to feel]
Social job: [How they want to be perceived]
Current alternative: [What they do today instead]
Switching trigger: [What makes them switch]
```

### 2 — Positioning Statement Structure
Every strategy includes a formal positioning statement:
```
For [target segment] who [problem/need],
[Product] is the [category] that [key differentiator]
unlike [competitive alternative], [proof point].
```

### 3 — Channel Unit Economics (required per channel)
```
Channel: [name]
CAC: $[amount]
LTV: $[amount]
LTV:CAC: [ratio]
Payback period: [months]
Scalability ceiling: [estimated max spend before diminishing returns]
```

### 4 — Growth Loop Classification
Identify which loop type applies to every growth strategy:
- **Viral loop:** K-factor target >1.0 for organic growth
- **Content loop:** Content investment → SEO/authority → acquisition
- **Sales loop:** Revenue → sales capacity → more revenue
- **Product loop:** Usage → engagement → referral → usage

### 5 — Measurement Framework
Every strategy includes:
- **Leading indicators** (predict future performance)
- **Lagging indicators** (confirm past performance)
- Cadence for review (weekly / monthly)
- Threshold that triggers strategic reassessment
