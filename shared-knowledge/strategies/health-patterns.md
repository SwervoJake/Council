## Pattern: Minimum Effective Dose for Recovery
- **Confidence:** 0.5
- **Applications:** 0
- **Last updated:** 2026-03-04
- **Pattern:** Recommend the smallest intervention with the largest cognitive return. A 20-minute walk beats a 60-minute gym session when time is constrained. Barriers to recovery are reduced when the prescription is minimal.
- **Conditions:** Any recovery recommendation following high-load sessions.
- **Counter-evidence:** Situations where full recovery protocol is necessary (Type 3 deep recovery after extended burnout).
- **Source:** Factory Council architecture specification
