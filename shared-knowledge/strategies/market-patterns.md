## Pattern: JTBD Before Channel
- **Confidence:** 0.5
- **Applications:** 0
- **Last updated:** 2026-03-04
- **Pattern:** Define the Jobs-to-be-Done persona before selecting channels or writing copy. Channel selection without persona clarity leads to correct mechanics applied to the wrong audience.
- **Conditions:** Any go-to-market initiative or marketing campaign.
- **Counter-evidence:** Existing channel with established persona — skip persona step if JTBD is already validated.
- **Source:** Factory Council architecture specification

## Pattern: Unit Economics Gate Before Scale
- **Confidence:** 0.5
- **Applications:** 0
- **Last updated:** 2026-03-04
- **Pattern:** Validate LTV:CAC >3× and payback <12 months before scaling any channel. Scaling negative unit economics accelerates cash burn without creating value.
- **Conditions:** Any channel investment decision involving meaningful spend increase.
- **Counter-evidence:** Deliberate land-grab strategy where LTV is uncertain but market timing is critical — document this as a strategic exception.
- **Source:** Factory Council architecture specification

## Pattern: Leading Indicators Before Lagging
- **Confidence:** 0.5
- **Applications:** 0
- **Last updated:** 2026-03-04
- **Pattern:** Define leading indicators (predict future performance) alongside lagging indicators. Lagging indicators tell you what happened. Leading indicators tell you what's about to happen, while there's still time to act.
- **Conditions:** Every growth strategy and channel investment.
- **Counter-evidence:** Situations where leading indicators are genuinely unknown — use lagging only and document the gap.
- **Source:** Factory Council architecture specification
