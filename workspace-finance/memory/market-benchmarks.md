## Benchmark Notes
*Context on when benchmarks apply vs. when to override:*

- Benchmarks are medians for comparable-stage companies. Top quartile is the target, not the ceiling.
- Early-stage benchmarks are less reliable — sample sizes are smaller.
- Sector matters more than stage for unit economics benchmarks.
